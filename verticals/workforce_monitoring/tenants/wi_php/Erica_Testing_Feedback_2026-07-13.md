QUESTIONS

When someone receives a link from a PHP, can we require demographic information? I think we need two link types, and I would like your view on this:

A registration link, which requires demographic information because it creates a record. Basically allowing someone to register themselvs with case manager review later.
A screening link, which does not require demographic information and can be sent to anyone, same front door used for our promotion separate from a PHP.

2. Title and credentials at enrollment, in the demographic information. NETWORK DIRECTORY

What is currently built as the evaluator directory, I would like to develop into something broader. I have attached a specification that sets out the full vision, and it supersedes the earlier evaluator directory materials I sent you.

These are the names I believe work well for the three distinct systems we will have:

Network Directory. The searchable listing of external entities, including evaluators, treatment facilities, laboratories, monitoring product vendors, clinicians, and support services.

Resource Library. The curated collection of educational and supportive content for participants and program staff, including papers, learning modules, pamphlets, tools, and links.

Document Repository. The internal service that stores, classifies, versions, and controls access to participant records and program documents, with OCR, retention, legal hold, and role based access controls. It is not participant facing.

The attached specification covers the network directory structure, verification, display and attribution rules, participant selection and disclosure, and the participant facing copy in the appendix.
