PI² Platform Overview
Predictive Performance Intelligence Infrastructure (PI²)

One front door for workforce stability and performance.

Insight gives any working professional — a clinician, a first responder, a licensed practitioner — a single, accessible way to check in, get a scored result, and connect to the right support. Behind that door is the infrastructure a Physician Health Program needs to monitor, predict, and stabilize.

What PI² is

The Performance Profile is the front door to PI² — Predictive Performance Intelligence Infrastructure: a single platform that does three jobs at once. It's a self-report pathway for anyone seeking support, a screening instrument that surfaces stability concerns early, and a performance baseline for people pursuing growth and optimization — across healthcare, first responders, and the wider workforce. One assessment in; screening, prediction, and matched support out.

1The Performance Profile — the single front door

A voluntary, confidential check-in designed to read as an accessible entry point, not a clinical intake. The same door serves someone addressing a concern, someone seeking support, and someone pursuing peak professional performance — without mislabeling any of them.

Component 1 · The stability read

Predictive Professional Stability Index (PPSI)

Eight domains of occupational stability — sleep, emotional exhaustion / burnout, isolation & support, recovery routines, cognitive load, and more — producing a 0–100 stability score and a color tier from green to red.

Component 2 · The optimization story

Foundations of Health Profile

Three lifestyle pillars not already covered by the PPSI — physical activity, nutrition, and substance use — each normalized 0–100. The highest is the dominant lifestyle driver: where intervention produces the greatest performance gain.

AUDIT-C NIDA Quick Screen HHS Physical Activity Guidelines Proprietary PPSI

All public-domain federal references plus the proprietary PPSI — no licensing dependencies.

Scored on the spot

Scan a QR code or open a link, answer the items in 10–15 minutes, and see a result immediately — stability tier with a breakdown by section, the dominant lifestyle driver with pillar bars, and matched resources. No app to install, no account required to try it.

▶ Open the live Performance Profile
Opens the working demo in a new tab — the same experience a participant would have from a QR code.

2Two tracks, one door

Reason for referral and service preferences each branch into a stability / wellness track and a performance optimization track, so acute stabilization and peak-performance coaching enter the same front door without being confused for one another. A licensure gate surfaces professional licensing fields only for licensed practitioners — everyone else completes without friction.

3From a score to ongoing engagement

A completed Profile isn't a dead end — it's the start of a relationship.

Take the assessment → Scored result → Matched resources → Participant portal → Ongoing engagement

Resources are matched to results — sleep content for a Sleep Stability concern, lifestyle-pillar content for the weakest Foundations pillar, general well-being content for everyone. And the door connects both ways to a Physician Health Program: a participant can opt in to PHP connection, or a PHP can refer someone in — registered and linked to that PHP's monitoring framework the moment the form is completed, with no manual re-entry.
