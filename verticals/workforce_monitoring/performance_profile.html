Your Performance Profile

A voluntary, confidential check-in designed to support the health, well-being, and professional performance of anyone in the workforce, whether you're addressing a concern, seeking support, or pursuing peak performance and professional development.

Your commitment to your own health and performance strengthens your work and benefits everyone who depends on you. Seeking support, whether to address a concern or to pursue continued growth, is a sign of professional strength.

It takes about 10–15 minutes. Honest responses produce the most useful results. This is a non-crisis service.

If you are in crisis, please reach out now:

  • Crisis Text Line — text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency Services — call 911
